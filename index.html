About

I am Wei Liu, a tenure-track assistant professor at the School of Management, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I received my PhD from UNC Chapel Hill under the supervision of Prof. Vinayak Deshpande and Prof. Vidyadhar Kulkarni. I was a postdoctoral fellow at Purdue University.

My research focuses on data-driven resource allocation in real-world operations, including airline, healthcare, and service systems, using optimization, queueing theory, and stochastic modeling.
